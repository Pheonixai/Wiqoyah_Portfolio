My Creative Philosophy

Hi I am Wiqs! I am a Graphics Designer committed to delivering purposeful design that communicates clearly and converts effectively. My process prioritizes speed and impact, translating marketing goals into visuals that capture attention.

I specialize in visual systems for promotional campaigns, generating everything from stunning Instagram graphics to print-ready event flyers. I focus on optimizing assets for specific platform requirements and audience engagement.

With primary expertise in Figma for rapid layout and component creation, and Canva for high-volume asset production and easy client handoff, I ensure quick turnaround times without compromising visual quality.
